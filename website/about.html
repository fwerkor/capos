ABOUT CAPOS

An open platform for NAS and edge computing

CapOS is maintained by the FWERKOR Team as an open-source operating system focused on practical server workflows, extensible architecture, and approachable system management.

Project goals

For makers and home labs

Provide an easy path to deploy storage, media, automation, and self-hosted services on affordable hardware.

For advanced users and teams

Preserve low-level control, scriptability, and modular expansion for professional and semi-professional environments.

Core principles

Open First

Transparent development and community collaboration are fundamental to the project.

Practical First

Design decisions prioritize reliability, maintainability, and real deployment value.

User First

Balance a smooth WebDesktop experience with strong CLI workflows for advanced operation.
